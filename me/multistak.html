Multistak

Juggle 3 games at once!

You’ve gained the ability to control the flow of time. You decided to play 3 puzzle games at once, because with great power comes great tomfoolery.

Multistak is a falling-blocks puzzle game where you fill solid rows of blocks to clear them. But here’s the catch: you play 3 games at once, and the two unfocused games are at a slower speed.

Game jam entry for Gotm.io Jam #2: Speed. Ranked 8/19.

If you like this game, please support Tetris by buying first-hand official entries, like Tetris 99 (NSW) or Tetris Effect (PS4, XB1, XBS, PC).
