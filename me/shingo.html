Shingo!

A Shavian word-guessing game you can play forever!

Guess the secret word, but written in Shavian.

Shingo! is a word-guessing game based on the TV show Lingo (and to some extent, its viral webapp ripoff Wordle).

Gameplay

You have 7 guesses to guess an English word written in Shavian. After each guess, each letter will be colored according to how close you were.

  • A gray letter (marked by √ó in Colorblind Mode) is not in the secret word.
  • A yellow letter (marked by ! in Colorblind Mode) is in the secret word, but in the wrong place.
  • A green letter (marked by ‚úì in Colorblind Mode) is in the secret word in the right spot.

Options

You can choose the number of letters in the word between 4 and 7 inclusive.

  • Hard mode forces all your guesses to take into account previous clues.
  • Always show first letter gives you the first letter when you start (as it is in Lingo). You can optionally turn this off for a more Wordle-like experience.
  • All words chooses the secret word from the entire word list, not just the more common words.
  • Colorblind mode adds an icon to the top right corner of each clue.
