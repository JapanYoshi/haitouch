Paintformer

Now with ink you can walk on!

Guide Jellysquid to the portal by platforming and drawing new platforms.

Paintformer is a Nintendo DS-style action platformer where you can paint your own platforms. Lead the Jellysquid into the portal at the end of the level, drawing your own platforms along the way!

The game requires a mouse or touchscreen. Optionally, you can use a keyboard or a gamepad to make platforming easier.

The mechanic of drawing physics objects with the cursor is based mainly on the Brain It On! app.

Game jam entry for Gotm.io Jam 14: Paint. $20 Prize winner for the Gameplay category! Ranked 3/6 by users.

Tip: If you prefer the look of mixels over a jagged but pixel-accurate look, turn on “High-resolution subpixel scaling” in the options.

Control schemes: mouse or touch required. Keyboard or gamepad optional.
