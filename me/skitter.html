Skitter Critter

D’aww, look at them go!

Guide the skittering critters down the lanes and into their desired exit, by making and breaking bridges.

Skitter Critter is a pointer-based action game, where you create and delete bridges to lead creatures to their corresponding exit.

Arcade Mode is a battle against the game clock, as the gameplay gets faster and faster as you go. Puzzle Mode is a test of foresight, as you cannot delete bridges.

The gameplay is based mainly on minigames from Super Mario 64 DS and New Super Mario Bros., specifically Connect the Characters and Mario’s Slides.

Game jam entry for Gotm.io Jam 6: Leading a Group. Ranked 2/5.

Control schemes: mouse, touch
