Skitter Critter

D’aww, look at them go!

Guide the skittering critters down the lanes and into their desired exit, by making and breaking bridges.

MalBots Begone! is a color-matching puzzle game where you connect 4 adjacent blocks of the same color, in order to defeat the creatures called MalBots.

The gameplay is based mainly on minigames from Super Mario 64 DS and New Super Mario Bros., specifically Connect the Characters and Mario’s Slides.

Game jam entry for Gotm.io Jam 6: Leading a Group. Ranking pending.

Control schemes: mouse, touch
