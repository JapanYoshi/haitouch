Connectiles

We all need connections.

Arrange the connected colored pieces into groups of the same color to pop them. Get big chain reactions to get a massive point bonus.

Connectiles is a color-matching puzzle game where you connect 4 adjacent blocks of the same color to clear them, ideally in a chain reaction. Blocks fall in linked pieces of 4 to 6, and fall as a connected group.

Game jam entry for Gotm.io Jam 5: Make It Random. Ranked 5/11.

The gameplay is based mainly on Puyo Puyo. The idea of connected pieces of different colors falling together was taken from Dr. Mario, and taken to a higher level. The idea of pieces rotating to slot into holes was taken from Tetris, specifically Guideline games like Tetris Worlds and later.

If you like this game, please support Puyo Puyo by buying first-hand official entries, such as Puyo Puyo Tetris, and SEGA AGES Puyo Puyo 1 and Puyo Puyo 2.

If you like this game, please support Dr. Mario by buying first-hand official entries... is what I would say if the latest entry weren’t a 3DS eShop exclusive from 2015.

If you like this game, please support Tetris by buying first-hand official entries, like Tetris 99 (NSW) or Tetris Effect (PS4, XB1, XBS, PC).

Control schemes: keyboard, gamepad
